GroupMe Case Study & Mid-Fi Mockup

GroupMe is a messaging platform most used by students to connect with classmates and peers. In addition to study groups, it is used in casual settings such as buy/sell/trade. Due to the nature of these large groups, oftentimes relevant messages get drowned out. As a result, many users struggle with organizing group chats and finding important notifications.
